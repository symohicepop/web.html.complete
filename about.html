Mission

The mission of Creative Lab is to create opportunities for African youth by training them on photography,filming and editing skills that prepare them for future of work by equipping them with marketable,industry driven skills. Our goal is to ensure that our talented youth are available and trained in order to meet the growing opportunities and needs of the ICT Industry in Africa. We also envision an explosion of technology innovation and the creation of successful tech ventures by local developers from across the country with a zeal for changing the world through harnessing the power of their technology training for social good.
